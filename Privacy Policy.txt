Privacy Policy for Supermarket Game
Effective Date: [11/03/2026]

1. Introduction
Welcome to Supermarket Game. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you play the game.

Because this game operates entirely within your web browser as a standalone web page, we do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any of your personal data.

2. Data We Collect and How It Is Stored
The game uses your browser's Local Storage to save your progress and preferences. This ensures you can close the game and return to it later without losing your place.

The specific data stored locally on your device includes:

Game Save Data: Your in-game money, gems, facility upgrades, unlocked maps, staff counts, and current time-of-day in the game.

Settings: Your preferences for Sound Effects (SFX) and Background Music (BGM).

Important: All of this data remains strictly on your personal device (computer, phone, or tablet). It is never uploaded to a cloud server, database, or shared with us or any third parties. If you clear your browser's cache or local storage, your save data will be permanently deleted.

3. Personal Information
We do not ask for, collect, or process any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, location, or IP address. There are no accounts to create and no login requirements.

4. Third-Party Services
While the game itself does not track you, it does load certain standard code libraries from third-party Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to make the game work. These include:

React & Babel (unpkg.com): To run the game's code.

Tailwind CSS: To style the game.

Google Fonts: To load the custom text fonts (Share Tech Mono and Nunito).

When your browser requests these files, those third-party services may log standard web request information (such as your IP address and browser type) in accordance with their own respective privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy
Because we do not collect any personal data, this game is entirely safe for children of all ages to play. We comply with the requirements of COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
If we update the game to include online features or leaderboards in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your local data is handled, please contact us
